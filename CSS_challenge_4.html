Cheese

Cheese is tasty. It's a delicious snack. It's is made with various types of milk, depending on what part of the world you live in. There are also dairy-free cheeses.

Cheese is very popular around the world, sometimes it is even used as a main ingredient in various dishes. For example, Mac and Cheese is a very popular and easy to prepare dish where the main ingredient is cheese!
